FAQ

Common Questions

Does this replace my internet provider?

No. Lighthouse works alongside your existing provider — Spectrum, AT&T, whoever you use. You keep everything exactly as it is. We just add a small device that monitors your connection and fixes problems when they come up.

Is this just a smart plug?

No. A smart plug can reboot your router on a timer, but it has no idea whether your internet is actually working. Lighthouse actively monitors your connection, diagnoses what's wrong, responds intelligently, and has a real person backing it up — all over a cellular connection that works even when your internet doesn't.

What if the problem is my ISP's fault?

We can't fix a neighborhood-wide outage, but we can tell you about it immediately — over cellular, before your guests have a chance to complain. You'll know it's an ISP issue, not your equipment, and we'll keep monitoring and alert you the moment service is restored.

How hard is it to install?

We ship the device pre-configured. You plug the Lighthouse device into a power outlet near your modem, connect it to your router with the included ethernet cable, and plug your modem and router into the smart plugs we include. That's it — no technician, no configuration, no technical knowledge required.

What does it cost after the pilot?

We're still finalizing pricing. Pilot participants will be the first to know, and will receive preferred pricing as an early adopter. There's no obligation to continue after the pilot ends.

Can Lighthouse see my guests' internet traffic?

No. Lighthouse only monitors whether your internet connection is up or down — it does not inspect, log, or have access to any of the traffic on your network. Your guests' browsing and activity is completely private.

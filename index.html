How It Works

Autonomous monitoring with human oversight

Lighthouse handles the mechanics. You stay informed without staying up.

Cellular-backed monitoring

Lighthouse uses its own cellular backup connection, which allows for alerts to be sent even when your internet is completely down. In our Fully Managed plan, it also allows our support team to remotely troubleshoot your internet outage.

Automatic healing, right sequence

Outage detected. Lighthouse automatically heals the connection — cycling the right device in the right order.

Alerts when detected and when fixed

You get an alert when the outage starts and another when internet returns. No action required on your end.

Human escalation if needed

If auto-heal doesn't resolve it, you're notified so you can intervene. With our Fully Managed plan, our support team jumps in to diagnose the problem for you.

Features

Built for properties. Runs on its own.

Cellular-independent monitoring

Watches over LTE — completely separate from your property's connection. Works even if your ISP is fully down.

Auto-healing

Smart auto-heal targets the right device. Most issues resolved before guests notice anything

Instant alerts

Real-time notification when an outage is detected, and again when internet is restored.

Zigbee plug control

Plug control runs over Zigbee radio — not WiFi. Reboots work even when the internet is fully down.

Online dashboard

See the status of all your properties in one place. History, uptime, and manual override controls.

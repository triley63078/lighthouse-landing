Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Built specifically for vacation rental property managers.

⚡

Automatic Power Cycling

Detects outages and reboots your modem and router automatically — typically restoring service within 3 minutes.

🔔

Instant Alerts

Get notified by email the moment an issue is detected at any of your properties, with details on what happened.

📡

Works Offline

Zigbee-based smart plug control works even when the internet is down, so Lighthouse can fix the very problem it detects.

📶

Always Reachable via LTE

The Lighthouse device has its own built-in cellular connection — completely separate from your property's internet. Even during a total outage, Lighthouse stays in contact with our servers so monitoring, alerts, and remote diagnostics never go dark. This connection is for the Lighthouse device only, not for your guests.

Secure by Design

All devices connect over an encrypted private network. Your property data never touches the public internet.

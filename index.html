How It Works

Autonomous monitoring with human oversight

Lighthouse handles the mechanics. You stay informed without staying up.

Cellular-backed monitoring

Lighthouse uses its own cellular connection for monitoring, alerting, and remote management. Because it's fully separate from your property's internet, outages are detected and reported even when your ISP is completely down.

Automatic healing, right sequence

Outage detected. Lighthouse automatically reboots the right device in the right order over Zigbee — no WiFi required.

Alerts when detected and when fixed

Email alerts keep you in the loop both when an issue occurs and when it's resolved. No action needed on your end.

Human escalation if needed

If auto-heal doesn't resolve it, you're notified immediately. On our Full-Service plan, our team jumps in to diagnose and coordinate a fix.

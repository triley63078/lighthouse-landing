How It Works

Autonomous monitoring with human oversight

Lighthouse handles the mechanics. You stay informed without staying up.

Cellular-backed monitoring

Lighthouse uses its own cellular backup connection, which allows for alerts to be sent even when your internet is completely down. In our Fully Managed plan, it also allows our support team to remotely troubleshoot your internet outage.

Automatic healing, right sequence

Outage detected. Lighthouse automatically heals the connection — cycling the right device in the right order.

Alerts when detected and when fixed

You get an alert when the outage starts and another when internet returns. No action required on your end.

Human escalation if needed

If auto-heal doesn't resolve it, you're notified so you can intervene. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Features
